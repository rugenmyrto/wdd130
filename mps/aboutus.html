About Us

New Era Auditing was established in 2011 with a mission to help individuals and businesses navigate their financial challenges. Over the years, we’ve built a reputation for providing reliable, tailored financial services. In 2018, we upgraded our office location after seven years of steady growth and success, marking the rebranding of our company to New Era Auditing along with our new logo. The founder, Rudina Zinxhiria, graduated from Ismail Qemali University in Vlore with a degree in Finance and Accounting and went on to complete a master's degree. Our team includes three dedicated professionals who share a passion for helping clients reach their financial goals. Today, we’re proud that 98% of the clients who start working with us continue to trust our services. Whether long-term or short-term, we’ve helped numerous businesses and individuals achieve their financial objectives. Our modern, high-end office reflects our commitment to providing professional and efficient services.
